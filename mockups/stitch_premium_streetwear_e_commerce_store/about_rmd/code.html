Forged in the Undergound.

RMD isn't just a label; it's a reaction. Born from the concrete reality of urban existence, we strip away the excess to reveal the raw structural integrity underneath. Our designs are tactical, aggressive, and uncompromisingly premium.

We don't follow seasons. We execute drops. Each piece is a calculated intervention in the luxury streetwear space, built for those who operate in the shadows and command attention in the light.

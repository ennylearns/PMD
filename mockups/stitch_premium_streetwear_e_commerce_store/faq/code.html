Shipping

ORDERS ARE TYPICALLY PROCESSED AND SHIPPED WITHIN 2-4 BUSINESS DAYS. DURING HIGH VOLUME RELEASES OR SALES, PROCESSING MAY TAKE UP TO 7-10 BUSINESS DAYS. YOU WILL RECEIVE A TRACKING NUMBER VIA EMAIL ONCE YOUR ORDER DISPATCHES.

YES, WE OFFER WORLDWIDE SHIPPING. INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING RATES ARE CALCULATED AT CHECKOUT BASED ON DESTINATION AND WEIGHT. PLEASE NOTE THAT CUSTOMS DUTIES AND TAXES ARE THE RESPONSIBILITY OF THE RECIPIENT AND ARE NOT INCLUDED IN THE SHIPPING COST.

Returns

WE ACCEPT RETURNS WITHIN 14 DAYS OF DELIVERY FOR STORE CREDIT ONLY. ITEMS MUST BE UNWORN, UNWASHED, AND IN THEIR ORIGINAL CONDITION WITH ALL TAGS ATTACHED. "FINAL SALE" ITEMS CANNOT BE RETURNED.

TO INITIATE A RETURN, PLEASE VISIT OUR RETURNS PORTAL AND ENTER YOUR ORDER NUMBER AND EMAIL ADDRESS. FOLLOW THE INSTRUCTIONS TO RECEIVE A RETURN SHIPPING LABEL (COST DEDUCTED FROM STORE CREDIT).

Sizing

OUR GARMENTS GENERALLY FEATURE AN OVERSIZED, BOXY FIT INTENDED FOR A STREETWEAR AESTHETIC. WE RECOMMEND ORDERING YOUR TRUE SIZE FOR THE INTENDED LOOK, OR SIZING DOWN FOR A MORE STANDARD FIT. DETAILED MEASUREMENTS ARE PROVIDED ON EACH PRODUCT PAGE.

Payments

WE ACCEPT ALL MAJOR CREDIT CARDS (VISA, MASTERCARD, AMEX), PAYPAL, APPLE PAY, AND SELECTED CRYPTOCURRENCIES (BTC, ETH) VIA COINBASE COMMERCE.

## What to build

Implement the email system to automatically email the customer when they place an order, when the status changes to Shipped, and when it changes to Delivered.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Email sent to customer upon order confirmation (PAID status)
- [ ] Email sent to customer when order status is changed to SHIPPED
- [ ] Email sent to customer when order status is changed to DELIVERED
- [ ] Email templates are responsive and include order summary
